  • 2008 Baby and Child
  • 2010 We shifted the focus, changed our name to RH and began our evolution into a luxury brand
  • 2011 First full-line design gallery opens in Houston
  • 2014 Begin curating wine label, Maisonry
  • 2015 RH Teen Launch

  • 2015 We open the Gallery at the Three Arts Club in Chicago, the first-of-its-kind concept, where we reimagine the retail experience with a restaurant and wine pairings to create a home-like atmosphere.
